Romane Temessek
France Artistic Swimming National Team
4 camps · 4–7 Aug · tap to filter
Romane Temessek is an international artistic swimmer representing France on the world stage and a member of the French Artistic Swimming National Team. A former World Youth Champion in solo and a World Championship finalist, she is part of an exciting new generation pushing the sport forward.

Known for her precision, expressive artistry, and powerful presence in the water, Romane brings elite-level experience and modern technique to her coaching. This August, she travels from France to the UK to inspire and develop the next generation of artistic swimmers.

Olivia Federici
Ex GB Artistic Swimming National Team
2 camps · 11–12 Aug · tap to filter
Olivia Federici is an Olympian and one of Great Britain's most accomplished artistic swimmers, having represented Team GB at the Olympic Games and multiple World and European Championships. A Commonwealth medalist and experienced international competitor, she has been a key figure in British artistic swimming for over a decade.

Known for her precision, creativity, and performance quality, Olivia brings a wealth of elite experience to her coaching. She is passionate about developing athletes' artistic expression, confidence, and love for the sport, inspiring the next generation to perform at their best.

Leilani Torres
International Artistic Swimming Consultant & World Aquatics Coaches Committee Member
2 camps · 14–15 Aug · tap to filter
Leilani Torres is an Olympian and internationally respected artistic swimming coach, mentor, and educator. She represented Puerto Rico at the Olympic Games and has since built a global coaching career, working with athletes and national teams across Europe, the Americas, and beyond.

Known for her technical expertise and ability to develop both athletes and coaches, Leilani now leads education programmes for World Aquatics and supports swimmers at all levels. Her coaching combines high-performance knowledge with a passion for helping athletes grow in confidence, skill, and understanding of the sport.

Kseniya Sydorenko
Ukrainian Olympian · Tokyo 2020
3 camps · 18–20 Aug · tap to filter
Kseniya Sydorenko is an Olympic medalist and one of Ukraine's most accomplished artistic swimmers, having competed at four Olympic Games and winning bronze at Tokyo 2020. She is also a multiple-time European champion and World Championship medallist, with over a decade of experience competing at the highest level of the sport.

Known for her consistency, strength, and technical excellence, Kseniya brings a wealth of elite performance knowledge to her coaching. She is passionate about sharing the discipline, detail, and mindset required to succeed at the top level of artistic swimming.

Where we train

The Venues

Tunbridge Wells
Tunbridge Wells Sports Centre
St John's Road, Royal Tunbridge Wells, TN4 9TX
Get directions ↗
Pool
25m competition pool, 5m deep dive pit & spectator seating. Dedicated space for ICON Series sessions.
Land space
Mirrored, air-conditioned dance studio space for land training throughout the day.
Getting there
5 mins from Tunbridge Wells train station. Car parking on site. Easy access from the A26.
Facilities
Changing rooms, showers, lockers, and a café on site for athletes and spectators.
London
London Aquatics Centre
Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, Stratford, London E20 2ZQ
Get directions ↗
Pool
The iconic location of the 2012 London Olympics. An extraordinary setting to train and learn in.
Land space
Purpose-built warm-up and conditioning areas within the Olympic Park complex, used by elite athletes year-round.
Getting there
Stratford station (Elizabeth line, Jubilee, Overground, DLR) — 10 mins walk. Bus stops directly outside. Limited parking on site.
Facilities
Full Olympic-standard changing facilities, lockers, café, and the incredible atmosphere of Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park.
Who can attend
Ages 10–18. Minimum 2 years artistic swimming experience required for all camps.
Group sizes
Maximum 20 athletes per day. 1 lead ICON coach plus 2 assistant coaches at every session.
ICON Badges
Every athlete who attends earns an official ICON Badge. Collect them and climb the In Synch 2026 Leaderboard.
